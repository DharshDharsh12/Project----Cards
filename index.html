The FAMOUS Festivals

Cherry Blossom

Cherry Blossom are a symbol of renewal and hope, and the coming of spring. Yoshino is known as the Cherry Blossom Capital of Japan. Small town Yoshino gets quite busy during cherry blossom season.

Holi Festival

The Holi Festival of Colours in India is a celebration of the victory of good evil, the destruction of the domoness Holika. It is celebrated every year on the day after the full moon in the Hindu month of Phalguna.

Lantern Festival

The Buddhist celebration is meant to cleanse the spirit from bad luck by sending lanterns to Buddha. People send lanterns to the sky and decorate their houses with flowers and candle which creates th lifting atmosphere.
